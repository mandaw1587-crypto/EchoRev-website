The Edge

The Triggers That Move Money

Psychology hasn't changed in 10,000 years. Only the tools have. We teach you to wield all seven.

Trigger 01

Scarcity & Urgency

Loss aversion is the most powerful force in human decision-making. When something is limited, perceived value spikes. We show you how to engineer legitimate scarcity into every offer.

Trigger 02

Authority

People follow experts. You'll learn to build rapid perceived authority in any niche — with zero credentials — using AI-assisted positioning, proof stacking, and strategic content.

Trigger 03

Social Proof

When people are uncertain, they look at what everyone else is doing. You'll learn how to collect, display, and amplify proof to eliminate hesitation before a buyer even asks.

Trigger 04

Reciprocity

Give strategically first. When you lead with genuine, specific value, people feel a psychological obligation to return it. The foundation of every high-converting lead magnet.

Trigger 05

Commitment

Small yeses create big yeses. Once someone agrees to a micro-action, they're psychologically primed to follow through on larger commitments. We build this ladder into your funnel.

Trigger 06

Identity & Liking

People buy from those they like — and from brands that reflect who they want to become. Your story, tone, and positioning are not cosmetic. They are the mechanism of the sale.
